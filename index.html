What's Iproc

e-Auction Module

e-Auction module provides procurement professionals with the most competitively price for their preferred products, pitching the suppliers directly against one another to see who can offer the lowest prices. It also streamlines the procurement process and saves time

Source to Contract Module

Provides an efficient digitization of a set of procurement processes in the sourching of products of services. If adopted, source-to-contract (S2C) module integrates procurement plasnning, request for quotation, bid evaluation, contract negotiation and award as well as contract management

Procure - To - Pay Module

Provides an efficient digitization of a set of procurement processes needed to obtain and manage material inputs necessary to manufacture a produce or to deliver a service. If adopted, the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) module integrates all transactional flow of data exchange with the suppliers for the entire to fulfilment process and its payment.

How's It Work?

4 steps to simplify your purchase order process

Setup Budgets

Setup budgets and invite colleagues, now you can setup approval rules and start requisition.

Users Submit

Buyer will submit requisition to eligible vendors based on purchase requisition from users.

Vendor Get Notified

Vendors get notified and able to submit quotation, including online negotiation with the buyer.

Evaluation And Awarded

Summary of quotation withreal-time view and get the best offers from vendors.
